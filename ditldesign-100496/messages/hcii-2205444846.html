Dear colleagues,


Due to multiple requests, the paper submission option for HCI International 2020 (HCII 2020) Conference remains open in order to give the opportunity to prospective authors to submit their work before the holiday period at the end of the year.


Review results will be provided within 15 working days from submission. After acceptance, the camera ready version of the full papers should be submitted by 31 January 2020, while authors will need to register for the Conference by 14 February 2020.


For more details, please refer to the paper proposal submissions web page.


HCII 2020 is organized under the auspices of 21 distinguished international boards and is expected to attract over 2,000 participants from more than 70 countries around the world. For the list of Affiliated Conferences / Thematic Areas, as well as their list of topics, please have a look at the Conference website.


The Conference Proceedings will be published by Springer in a multi-volume set. Papers will appear in volumes of the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) and Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence (LNAI) series. Poster Extended Abstracts will be published in the form of short papers in the Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series.


Conference contact: Program Administration.
