TaskRabbit 
http://nextjuggernaut.com/blog/how-task-rabbit-works-insights-into-business-revenue-model/

TaskRabbit provides a platform for people looking for easy to do simple freelance jobs and busy people wanting to outsource easy tasks. TaskRabbit leverages the crowdsourcing trend, in which private consumers engage in decentralizing tasks they do not like to do. The service started its operations in 2008 and expanded to 19 cities in the United States and London as well as set up a section of virtual tasks that can be performed anywhere.
