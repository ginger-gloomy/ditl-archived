199 submissions to this track were peer reviewed. Each submission received two reviews; and one of the two reviewers provided a meta-review. ------ REVIEWS -------

----------------------------------------------------------------

AC review
score 4/5

  Overall Rating

    Probably accept: I would argue for accepting this paper.

  Expertise

    Expert

  Review, for AC

    This paper describes BusMyFriend, a service aiming at supporting the travel
    experience of people with visual impairments in bus transportation in Taipei City.
    The authors’ goal is to support the whole experience rather than specific steps of
    what makes an entire journey. While this paper describes the design of their
    system, the authors did a lot of work that is only very briefly summarized. Still,
    Table 1 resulting from the initial interviews was useful to structure the paper.

    My main comment is that even though the authors mention they tried to ensure their
    implementation is feasible, it is not clear how it is integrated with existing
    infrastructure. For instance, is the authors’ implementation already integrated
    with the systems used by the bus driver? How easy was it / would it be? How
    feasible would it be to install the acrylic tactile indicators in all stops? This
    is not specified and therefore it is difficult to grasp if it is really highly
    feasible to be implemented in the field (I hope it is, but it is not clear).

    I also think proofreading the paper before publication would improve the quality
    of this paper, as there are a few typos or grammatical mistakes.

    META:
    Overall,  both reviewers argue for accepting this paper, even though the amount of
    work seems more appropriate for a full paper submission (AC2). We agree the design
    of BusMyFriend adds value by itself as a WiP (AC2), but it would be interesting to
    know further information about how it can be integrated with current systems
    (e.g., with bus drivers).

----------------------------------------------------------------

2AC review
score 4/5

  Overall Rating

    Probably accept: I would argue for accepting this paper.

  Expertise

    Expert

  Review, for 2AC

    This paper presents a low-cost service that facilitates bus journeys for people
    with low vision. The paper focuses mostly on the design of the service, but also
    mentions user studies that have been carried out to understand the user needs and
    to evaluate the service. I am quite enthusiastic about this paper and about the
    service that the authors design. I actually find it a pity that the authors did
    not submit a full paper about their work, as I think that the methods and findings
    of the user studies would be very interesting to present at DIS. Because these
    studies are mentioned but not discussed in detail, the focus of the current paper
    is a bit unclear. Nevertheless, I think that presenting the concept of BusMyFriend
    is valuable enough in itself for acceptance as a work in progress paper.
