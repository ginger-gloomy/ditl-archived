[Test] 使用者端（學生）量化測試計畫

目的
  • 了解各功能之實際需求與實用性（ＱＱ其實應該倒過來做）
  • 收集受訪者，未來做質化訪談
  • 讓後台能收到資料，讓其他stakeholders能真正使用平台
設置地點
  • 系統能穩定運作的機器：電資大樓7F 8F
  • 有迫切裝水需求的情境：體育館（詢問是否機器可以穩定運作）
image.png 701 KB • Download
研究方法
  • 使用Google Analytics(GA) 量化分析使用者行為
    • 點擊「回報問題」的比例，以及完成填寫的比例
    • 點擊「附近飲水機」的比例
    • 點擊「追蹤飲水機」的比例
  • 在PWA中加入問卷流程，收集回饋（要想一下flow怎樣比較順，增加填答率）
    • 點擊「回報問題」後，詢問是否有意願接受訪談
    • 點擊「附近飲水機」後，詢問是否有成功裝到水
    • 點擊「追蹤飲水機」後，詢問追蹤的原因
    • 詢問五星滿意度，以及收集回饋
實驗準備
  • QRcode掃描立牌（或其他形式），設計多款，測試哪種方式比較能吸引人掃描使用

Study of In-App Feedback


Why in-app feedback?
  • Asking a user for feedback on a particular feature or experience while they’re actively using your app will not only increase your response rate but you’ll receive more accurate feedback because your users are highly engaged. This fresh perspective can be particularly helpful!
  • In-app feedback is more accurate. Human memory is imperfect, so it’s best not to rely on recall. 
  • Target Specific Users
  • [Advanced] We can dynamically improve the in-app experience.
  • [Advanced] Improve the prediction accuracy rate. (ex. Grammarly, Netflix.)

Purpose
- increase the response rate
- get higher quality feedback

Tactic
  • Timing is everything. Wrapping feedback into a practical task that users want or need to complete is a great way to increase your response rate.
  • The more simple your questions and method of collection are, the more likely you are to receive helpful feedback.
  • Draw users in with the option to provide painless, concise feedback, and when they take you up on it, ask for more information.
